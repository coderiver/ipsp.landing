Business driven Anti-Fraud settings

At iPSP.com we use proprietory anti-fraud solution that may be fine-tuned for each merchant.

Chargebacks and fraudulent transactions are low to none. Fine-tuning anti-fraud solution allows us to recover and successfully process 5% to 20% of transactions that would otherwise considered fraudulent. Imagine changes to your revenue.

iPSP.com may use additional external filters at merchant request alongside it's own solution, or completely substituting it.

Rebill

Rebill is also known as recurrent or subscription billing. This feature is important for service providers, that charge client per period (say - monthly or quarterly).

Whether merchant sells content on web site or provides SaaS service or delivers regular in-app purchases - we will make sure client pays on schedule.
